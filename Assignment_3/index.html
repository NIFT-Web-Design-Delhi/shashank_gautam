Connect with Artisans

Our focus group of artisans has been the Bhat family who has been practicing the craft of Kathputli for seven generations. For a family like theirs, Kathputli is their passion and their bread & butter. They have never known anything beyond the craft to pursue as a career. We, the team of Kaasth Katha had the privilege to speak with Vinod and Arjun Bhat of the family. In our fruitful discussion they explained to us the technical as well as creative process of the Kathputli craft, reminisced about their best experiences as an artisan group and opened up to us about their post-pandemic struggles. This website is a small initiative to get them the reach, coverage and appreciation they deserve.

"A family of acclaimed puppeteers that has been performing the art of puppetry for the last several generations, Vinod Bhat, Arjun Bhat, Jiten Bhat and Shravan Bhat have performed for many national and international fairs and have been accoladed with several state and national awards.""
